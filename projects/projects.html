PAST INTERESTS

During my Ph.D. studies, I investigated emergent phenomena in ultracold quantum gases. To this end, I used advanced electro and magneto-optical techniques to trap, cool, and manipulate ultracold matter with high precision

For my B.S. dissertation, I used scanning tunneling microscopy to measure, analyze, and characterize the surface of thin conductive films. This work aimed to investigate the effects of electron-surface scattering on the electronic transport in metals.
