David Chen

Postdoctoral researcher at the Myers Lab, Max Planck Institute for Cell Biology and Genetics, Dresden, Germany

I am currently designing and building a two-photon microscope capable of imaging large volumes of fixed tissue –as large as 1 cm^3– with sub-micron resolution and in-situ. This novel technology will be used to understand how cells form tissue at large scales in 3D.

As part of this project, I have established a new advanced microscopy laboratory at the MPI-CBG.
